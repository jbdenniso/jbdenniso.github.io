Course Description
======
This course provides a broad overview of the structures of the brain and their function. Gross anatomy of
the  central nervous system will be covered. The organization of the major neural systems underlying
sensory, motor, and cognitive function will be emphasized. Within each topic, we will discuss dysfunction
of the nervous system and how neuroanatomical changes can produce specific neurological disorders.
